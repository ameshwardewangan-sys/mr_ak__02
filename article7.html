⚠️ Common Cyber Threats

📧 Phishing

Fake emails or messages used to steal passwords and personal data.

🦠 Malware

Viruses and harmful software that damage systems.

🔐 Ransomware

Locks your data and demands money to unlock it.

🌐 DDoS Attack

Overloads websites with traffic to crash them.
